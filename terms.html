Terms and Conditions

Please read before placing an order:

  • Make sure to check out my Instagram story or message me directly on Instagram to discover my availability for taking on commissioned work.
  • Orders will be completed within the estimated time or sooner.
  • Commissions are placed through the website and handled via email.
  • One email format is intended for a single image order. If you have multiple orders, please fill out the format on the commission page again and submit it separately.
  • Attach reference images and proof of payment in the email.
  • Payment is only accepted through PayPal or the bank account listed on the commission page.
  • Commissions without reference photos and proof of payment will not be processed.
  • If unavailable, you can request a cancellation using the provided contact.
  • Project progress will be updated via email, allowing for feedback.
  • Please ensure that the amount on the proof of transfer is equal to or less than the nominal price on the order to avoid any processing delays. Thank you!
